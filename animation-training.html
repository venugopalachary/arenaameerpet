Animation your way to success

Animation opens up a world of imagination for students, offering immense opportunities for them to flex their creative muscles. Students can create and build entire characters, cartoons and other inanimate objects right from scratch. There is no limit to the things that can be created, ranging from humans to robots to extinct animals like mammoths as well as creatures from stories and fables like fairies and monsters.

Top animation studios like Pixar, Disney, Dreamworks and Studio Ghibli are now producing quality content on an enormous scale, and the demand for animated content and animators is only increasing! The animation industry is now looking for talented minds that are capable of taking the industry’s legancy forward.

The Animation Prime is a comprehensive training program in the fundamentals and techniques of animation that provides comprehensive understanding of the latest software and tools used in the process of animation in the industry. The program readies students for an animation career, where they can set their imagination free.

On completion, this program opens up a wide range of job roles for the students:

  • Graphic Designer
  • Lighting Artist
  • Compositor
  • 3D Animator
  • Video Editor
  • Rendering Artist
  • Texturing Artist
  • Rigging Artist
  • 3D Modeler
